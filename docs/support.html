Common questions

How do I start a timer?

Click the Deepflow icon in your Mac's menu bar to open the popover, set your time (drag the digits or pick a preset), and hit Start. Deepflow counts down right in the menu bar.

Where is my focus data stored?

Entirely on your Mac — nothing is uploaded anywhere. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my Deepflow Pro purchase?

Open Deepflow's Settings → Deepflow Pro and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you bought it with.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not inside Deepflow. On your Mac: open the App Store → click your name → Account Settings → Subscriptions → select Deepflow. You can also manage it on an iPhone/iPad under Settings → your name → Subscriptions.

The timer and stopwatch — are they really free?

Yes. The core timer and stopwatch are free forever, with no account and no lock. Deepflow Pro only adds the advanced focus stats, goals, tags, and history tools.
